2. NVTの目的

NVTは、Novet内で創作活動に対する価値体験を提供するために導入しています。 作品投稿、有料作品、報酬、外部ウォレット連携などを通じて、クリエイターの活動を支援することを目的としています。

Novetにおける価値の中心は、トークン価格ではなく、サービスの品質、投稿体験、読者とのつながり、 AIイラストや保管庫などの創作支援機能、そして作品そのものにあります。

3. NVTでできること

  • Novet内での投稿報酬として受け取る
  • 有料作品の購入に利用する
  • ユーザーのDynamicウォレットで保有する
  • 外部ウォレットへ出金する
  • Novet内でNPを買う機能に利用する

利用できる機能は、サービスの公開状況、メンテナンス、アカウント状態、ウォレット状態、ネットワーク状況により制限される場合があります。

English Overview

NovetCoin (NVT / NOVET) is a Polygon-based utility token used within the Novet creative posting platform. Novet is designed as a creative platform where users can publish novels and illustration-based works, access paid works, and use platform features related to creator activity.

NVT is used for creator rewards, paid creative works, purchasing Novet Points (NP), and wallet-based deposit and withdrawal functions. Novet Points (NP) are internal platform points used for service fees and platform features within Novet.

NovetCoin is not presented as an investment product, security, or guaranteed-value asset. The project does not guarantee market price, liquidity, exchange listing, profit, or redemption value for NVT. Users should always verify the official contract address before interacting with NVT on external wallets or block explorers.

  • Network: Polygon PoS
  • Contract Address: 0x2bCAbF131ab1D3620914f17b576BD3d6fA76812e

  • Primary Use: Utility token for creator rewards, paid works, NP purchase, and wallet withdrawal functions on Novet.

Token Utility / Use Cases

NovetCoin / NVT is designed as a utility token for the Novet creative posting platform. It is intended to support creator activity, paid creative works, and wallet-based platform functions.

  • Creator Rewards: NVT may be distributed as a platform reward for creators based on activity, contribution, or campaign rules defined by Novet. Reward rules may include contribution-based limits such as text volume and daily reward caps.
  • Paid Creative Works: NVT may be used in connection with paid novels, illustration-based works, and other creator-driven digital content on Novet.
  • Purchasing Novet Points: Users may use NVT to purchase Novet Points (NP), which are internal platform points used for service fees and platform features.
  • Wallet Deposit and Withdrawal: NVT may be deposited to or withdrawn from supported wallets through Novet's wallet-related functions.
  • Liquidity Support: The operator may provide limited liquidity support or reference market data to support platform-related utility functions. This does not represent a guarantee of market price, liquidity, profit, or redemption value.

NVT does not represent ownership of Novet, voting rights, equity, revenue share, or a claim against the operator. NVT is not presented as an investment product, and Novet does not guarantee market price, liquidity, profit, exchange listing, or redemption value.
